Project info:

Vorace is a roguelite platformer 2D game where you fight your way across a level to reach the end and try to get to the boss. All while gaining money on the way to futher upgrade yourself for future run

This game was made in 2023 and was my final project of my 1st year

What I've done:

In this project I've done a camera system that followed the player and was able to take custom limit bound to not show outside of the level

I've made the final boss with his custom AI and patern that attacked the player with different attack in function of the distance to the player

I've done the ingame HUD with some effect on the life bar and mana bar

I've made the interaction to switch menu in the main menu
