Project info:

TimeCapsule is a puzzle game where you change temporality to solve the puzzle and explore your own house when you were a kid and an adult

This game was made in 2025 and was my final project of my 3rd year

What I've done:

In this project I've started by prototyping some shaders to give an atmosphere to the game

I've followed up by setting up a basic cinematic for the game intro combined with a shader to simulate a disorienting awakening

I've also worked on some puzzle where you play with physics and need to input sequences

I've also played with particle making some dust particle for a cut part of the game, particle for when you complete a puzzle and wind particle for the garden outside

I've manage to set 3d element in the ui

I've try to make stencil buffer to try and make window for the house without cutting a hole in the geometry

I was in charge of the lightmap in the house and made a script to swap lightmap when changing temporality

I've also helped with the shader to make transition between temporality
